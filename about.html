Our Story

A family farm rooted in Rosharon.

Capistrano Farms was founded in 2005 in Rosharon, Texas, where it began as a cattle farm. As nearby communities have grown, the farm has adapted to meet the needs of the area by growing trees for new homes, neighborhoods, and landscape projects.

Today, we work with landscapers and homeowners, providing quality trees that help new properties feel established. As a family-owned business, everything we do is rooted in the values of home, land, and community.
